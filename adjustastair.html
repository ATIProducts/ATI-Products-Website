Frequently Asked Questions

AdjustaStairs FAQ

Everything you need to know about AdjustaStairs from ATI Construction Products.

What is the AdjustaStairs system?

AdjustaStairs is an adjustable aluminum temporary stair system manufactured by ATI Construction Products, LLC in Arvada, Colorado. It provides safe, OSHA 1926.1051-compliant access to roofs and elevated areas on construction sites. The system adjusts to fit different roof pitches and heights without tools, deploys in minutes, and is lightweight enough for one or two workers to install.

Is AdjustaStairs OSHA compliant?

Yes. AdjustaStairs is engineered to meet OSHA 1926.1051 standards for stairways on construction sites. It provides a safer alternative to ladders for workers who need frequent roof access, and meets the OSHA requirement for stairways when elevation changes are 19 inches or greater.

What heights does AdjustaStairs accommodate?

AdjustaStairs is available in multiple height configurations to accommodate a wide range of roof elevations and construction site conditions. Contact ATI Construction Products at (800) 360-6115 for specific height range specifications and to find the right configuration for your project.

How quickly can I get an AdjustaStairs quote?

ATI Construction Products offers same-day quotes on AdjustaStairs systems. Contact us before 2 PM Mountain Time at (800) 360-6115 or fill out the online form and we will respond the same business day. We ship nationwide from Arvada, Colorado.

Does AdjustaStairs ship nationwide?

Yes. ATI Construction Products ships AdjustaStairs systems to all 50 states from our headquarters in Arvada, Colorado. We have served general contractors, roofing companies, and construction crews across the country since 1999.

What is the difference between AdjustaStairs, AdaptaSteps and AdaptaSpan?

AdjustaStairs is the primary stair tower system for roof access. AdaptaSteps provides modular step solutions for varied site conditions. AdaptaSpan bridges horizontal gaps. Contact ATI for a comparison based on your specific project needs.
