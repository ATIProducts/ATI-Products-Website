FAQ

Lead Generation Questions Answered

What lead generation services does ATI Business Essentials offer?

ATI Business Essentials offers lead capture automation, call tracking, bid pipeline management, qualified lead tagging, CRM integration, and lead sync to Google Ads.

What is the ATI Business Essentials Marketing Hub?

The Marketing Hub is an all-in-one platform including lead generation, Google Ads management, SEO, social media, email, reputation management, and reporting — built specifically for service businesses and contractors.

Call tracking, form capture, and chat integration record every lead from every source. Leads are tagged by source, quality, and status, and flow into your bid pipeline for full revenue attribution.

Yes. ATI Business Essentials integrates with many popular CRM platforms. Our team will connect your existing tools during onboarding.
